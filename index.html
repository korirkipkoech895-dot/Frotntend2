Loan Rules

Loan Rules & Guidelines

1. Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a Kenyan citizen aged 18 years or older
  • Must have a valid national ID card
  • Must have an active phone number registered in your name
  • Must have a verifiable source of income
  • Must not be listed on CRB with negative credit history

2. Loan Amounts & Terms

  • Minimum loan amount: KSh 6,000
  • Maximum loan amount: KSh 100,000
  • Repayment period: 1 to 12 months
  • Interest rate: 1.99% per month
  • Processing fee: 2% of loan amount

3. Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form with accurate information
  2. Submit required documents (ID, proof of income)
  3. Wait for instant automated approval (usually within minutes)
  4. Review and accept loan offer
  5. Pay processing fee to release funds
  6. Receive loan disbursement to your chosen method

4. Repayment Rules

  • Repayment is due on the agreed date in your loan agreement
  • Early repayment is allowed without penalties
  • Late payments incur additional charges of 5% per week
  • Failed payments will be reported to Credit Reference Bureaus
  • Partial payments are accepted but must clear the full amount by due date

5. Loan Limit Increases

Your loan limit can increase based on:

  • Successful repayment of previous loans
  • Timely payments without defaults
  • Improved credit score
  • Increased income verification

6. Prohibited Uses

Loan funds may not be used for:

  • Illegal activities
  • Gambling or speculative investments
  • Purchasing illegal substances
  • Any activity that violates Kenyan law

7. Default Consequences

Failure to repay your loan may result in:

  • Negative credit bureau listing
  • Legal action and debt collection
  • Inability to access future loans
  • Additional penalty charges
  • Contacts to your listed references

8. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Notify us of any changes to your contact details
  • Ensure sufficient funds for repayment
  • Contact us immediately if you face repayment difficulties
  • Read and understand the loan agreement before accepting

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, secure access to your account, and loan application processing.

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits, time spent, and navigation patterns.

3. Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide enhanced, personalized features.

  • Language preferences

  • Facebook Pixel
