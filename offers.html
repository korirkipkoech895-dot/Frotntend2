Why Processing Fee?

Our 0.85% processing fee is one of the lowest in the market and covers essential administrative costs to ensure the security of your loan.

Example: For a KSh 50,000 loan, you pay only KSh 425 as processing fee.

What the fee covers:

  • Comprehensive credit verification and assessment
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems
  • Document processing and verification
  • Bank-grade 256-bit encryption security
  • Instant fund disbursement to your account

Why we keep it low: We believe in fair, transparent lending. Our 0.85% fee is significantly lower than the industry average of 2-5%, making loans more affordable for you.
