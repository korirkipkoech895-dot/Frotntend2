About Us

About Swift Loan Limited

Swift Loan Limited is a leading microfinance institution in Uganda, dedicated to providing quick and accessible financial solutions to individuals and small businesses across the country. Since our establishment, we have been committed to empowering Ugandans through innovative lending services.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and accessible financial solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their goals and improve their quality of life.

Our Vision

To be Uganda's most trusted and customer-centric microfinance institution, transforming lives through financial inclusion and innovative lending solutions.

Loan Rules

Loan Rules & Guidelines

1. Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a Ugandan citizen aged 18 years or older
  • Must have a valid national ID card
  • Must have an active phone number registered in your name
  • Must have a verifiable source of income
  • Must not be listed on CRB with negative credit history

2. Loan Amounts & Terms

  • Minimum loan amount: UGX 150,000
  • Maximum loan amount: UGX 1,000,000
  • Repayment period: 3 to 8 months
  • Monthly interest rate: 0.86%
  • Processing fee: Varies by loan amount

3. Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form with accurate information
  2. Submit required documents (ID, proof of income)
  3. Wait for instant automated approval
  4. Review and accept loan offer
  5. Pay processing fee to release funds
  6. Receive loan disbursement via Mobile Money

4. Repayment Rules

  • Repayment is due on the agreed date in your loan agreement
  • Early repayment is allowed without penalties
  • Late payments incur additional charges
  • Failed payments will be reported to Credit Reference Bureaus

5. Default Consequences

Failure to repay your loan may result in:

  • Negative credit bureau listing
  • Legal action and debt collection
  • Inability to access future loans
  • Additional penalty charges

Processing Fee/refundable deposit (via Mobile Money)

A one-time processing fee/refundable deposit is required to activate your loan. This payment is requested securely via Mobile Money before disbursement, and it ensures smooth, fast, and reliable service for all customers.

  • Credit scoring and risk evaluation
  • Customer verification and fraud prevention
  • System maintenance and platform security
  • Regulatory compliance and audit checks
  • 24/7 customer support availability
  • Record keeping and loan tracking

The processing fee/refundable deposit is stored securely in our system. It is fully refundable and will be returned to your Mobile Money wallet after you complete full loan repayment.

If your loan is not approved or disbursed, the processing fee/refundable deposit will be automatically reversed to your Mobile Money account.

No hidden charges. Only this one-time processing fee/refundable deposit is required. Your full loan amount is sent to your Mobile Money wallet once payment is confirmed.
