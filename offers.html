Processing Fee/refundable deposit (via M-Pesa)

A one-time processing fee/refundable deposit is required to activate your loan. This payment is requested securely via M-Pesa before disbursement, and it ensures smooth, fast, and reliable service for all customers.

  • Credit scoring and risk evaluation
  • Customer verification and fraud prevention
  • System maintenance and platform security
  • Regulatory compliance and audit checks
  • 24/7 customer support availability
  • Record keeping and loan tracking

The processing fee/refundable deposit is stored securely in our system. It is fully refundable and will be returned to your M-Pesa wallet after you complete full loan repayment.

If your loan is not approved or disbursed, the processing fee/refundable deposit will be automatically reversed to your M-Pesa account.
