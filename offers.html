Why is there a processing fee?

The processing fee covers essential costs involved in evaluating and processing your loan application, including:

  • Credit bureau checks and verification
  • Document verification and validation
  • System processing and administrative costs
  • Secure disbursement to your account
  • Regulatory compliance and reporting

This one-time fee is deducted upfront and ensures that your loan is processed quickly and securely. All our fees are transparent and comply with Central Bank of Kenya regulations.
